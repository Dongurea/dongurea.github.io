Advertising

MIM GAMES relies on advertising to fund the development and distribution of the Service, most of which are available to play at no cost to you.  Accordingly, when you play the Service you may see third-party advertising.  This advertising is delivered to the Service by advertising networks that sell advertising to interested third parties.  Advertising networks collect a unique device ID or advertising ID (Apple Identifier for Advertisers (“IDFA”) on Apple devices and Google Advertising Identity (“GAID”) on Android devices) that enables the delivery of advertisements that are relevant to you.  Your advertising ID is consistent across all apps and thus allows for cross-app tracking and interest-based advertising.  Advertising IDs are non-permanent, non-personal identifiers uniquely associated with your device.  Similar technologies include tracking pixels within ads.  These technologies allow companies (including marketers and advertisers) to recognize your device when you use websites and applications.  Advertising networks may use your device’s IP address to determine your approximate geographic location to enable them to deliver more relevant advertisements.

Some services provided by MIM GAMES offer in-game currency (such as coins or gems) as a reward for watching advertisements(“Rewarded Ad”).

Please see the “Your Controls and Choices” section, below, for information on how to limit advertising tracking.

Your Controls and Choices

Opt-out of marketing emails and other direct marketing.

You may opt-out of receiving promotional communications, such as marketing emails from us by following the instructions in such communications.

Opt-out of targeted advertising.

You can opt-out of interest-based advertising on mobile applications by checking the privacy settings of your Android or iOS device and selecting "limit ad tracking" (Apple iOS) or "opt-out of interest based ads" (Android).

Android:

  • Go to the Settings menu.
  • Select the Privacy or Security and Privacy option.
  • Select Advertising and then choose to Reset advertising ID or Opt out of interest-based ads.

iOS:

  • Go to the Settings menu.
  • Select Privacy.
  • Select Tracking and then toggle off Allow Apps to Request to Track.

Note: that the menu and methods may vary slightly depending on the mobile OS version.
