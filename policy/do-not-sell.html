Do Not Sell My Personal Information

The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) came into effect on January 1, 2020 and it reinforces the protection of personal information for California residents. MIM GAMES is committed to safeguarding personal information in compliance with the CCPA.

MIM GAMES processes personal information as described in its Privacy Policy. Except as described in these notices, MIM GAMES does not sell your personal information for monetary or other valuable consideration.

The CCPA provides that California residents may opt out of the sale of their personal information. We may ask you to provide additional personal information so that we can properly identify you in our dataset and to track compliance with your opt-out request. We will only use personal information provided in an opt-out request to review and comply with the request. If you choose not to provide this information, we may only be able to process your request to the extent we are able to identify you in our data systems and authenticate you as authorized to receive the information. If you are a California Resident and would like to exercise this right, please send an email to legal@mimgames.co.
